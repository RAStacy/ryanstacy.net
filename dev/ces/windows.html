Our Process

Design Consultation

The products we carry are extraordinary vinyl windows that come in all shapes and sizes. That's why our design consultant will meet with you in your home to review your options and create the best plan for your success. Our design consultants will listen and discuss your options, show you product samples, and give a FREE QUOTE based on what was discussed.

Other things we will discuss ...

  • What is Low E glass to block UV?
  • Grids options for your home?
  • What color vinyl matches your taste?
  • What is your price point and expectations?

Vinyl Window are: little to no maintenance, no painting or staining needed. Low e-coating and argon gas create a strong energy efficient environment.

Our vinyl windows offer thinner frames without sacrificing rigidity. Glass is maximized more than most replacement windows and the amazing frame design provides highest energy efficiency that far outperforms ENERGY STAR® standards. As a result, heating and cooling costs are reduced.

Simonton's Auto Sense Lock activates when closed and gives the homeowner full control of how the window is functioning at that time. The lock surpasses the nation’s strictest forced entry code requirements. The sleek, low-profile design doesn’t intrude or take away from the sightline of the window.

Production

After style has been chosen our design consultant will take exact measurements of your windows and place an order for custom made windows just for your home.

Windows are then received at our facility and a installation appointment is made with the homeowner.Our installers are clean, polite, and professional. The sanctity of a home is always preserved.
